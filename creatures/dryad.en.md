## Taxonomy

### Types

- **Daphnaie** - a nymph of a laurel tree.
- **Epimeliad** (plural **Epimelides**, also **Maliades**, **Meliades**) - a nymph of an apple or other fruit tree.
- **Hamadryad** - a nymph living inside a tree.
- **Meliae** - a nymph of an ash tree.
